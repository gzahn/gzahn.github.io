Biological Computing: From Laptop to Cluster

Introduces basic data skills for biologists with a focus on genetic sequence data, command-line usage, and pipeline development. Develops competence in interacting with a remote high-performance computing environment. Applies the principles of project organization and reproducibility to create an analysis pipeline. Develops competence in installing, parsing, and linking novel bioinformatics tools.
